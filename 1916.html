Archduke Franz Ferdinand was assassinated which aroused the war. Then, on July 28th Austria-Hungary declared war on Serbia. This began World War I. In August Germany invaded Luxembourg and Belgium and France invaded Alsace. British forces entered France. Great Britain, Russia, Italy, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Rhodesia, Romania, Greece, France, Belgium, United States, Canada, Serbia, India, Portugal, Montenegro, and Poland all allied against Germany. Austria-Hungary invaded Russia next. In the first battle of the Marne allied forces halted German advances into France.
